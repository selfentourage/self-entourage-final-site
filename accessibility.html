Accessibility Statement

Self Entourage is committed to ensuring that our website and services are accessible to all.

Our Commitment

We strive to make our website and digital products usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. Our goal is to comply with relevant standards such as the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Measures We Take

To support accessibility, Self Entourage:

  • Uses clear, high‑contrast colour palettes
  • Provides alternative text for meaningful images
  • Designs consistent navigation and headings
  • Ensures forms are labelled and keyboard accessible
  • Tests pages with assistive technologies

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review and update our site to improve usability. If you encounter any barriers, please let us know.

Contact

If you have suggestions or encounter accessibility issues, please contact us at accessibility@selfentourage.com. We value your feedback and will work to address concerns promptly.
