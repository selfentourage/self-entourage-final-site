About Self Entourage

Self Entourage is an AI systems design, education and implementation business based in Aurora, Colorado. Our mission is to empower sovereign entrepreneurs with clear AI frameworks, robust operating systems and hands‑on support.

Our story

We started as a small collective of technologists and educators who believed that cutting‑edge AI should be accessible, ethical and empowering. Over the years we've helped entrepreneurs, creators and organisations build systems that free them from operational chaos and enable them to focus on their mission.

We teach AI clearly, build reusable systems and provide hands‑on consulting. Every product we offer is rooted in our belief that a business should be a self‑improving machine rather than a constant hustle.

Our values

  • Sovereignty – own your data, systems and outcomes
  • Clarity – demystify AI and make it actionable
  • Integrity – honour promises and protect intellectual property
  • Empathy – design systems that serve real people

We serve clients throughout the Denver metropolitan area. Meetings are available by appointment only—please schedule a consultation before visiting so we can prepare for you.
