FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about Self Entourage, product delivery, AI system builds, appointments, refunds, and the best way to start.

What is Self Entourage?
Self Entourage is an AI systems design, education, and implementation company that helps people learn AI, organize ideas, build workflows, and deploy practical business systems.
What is Truth OS?
Truth OS is the operating logic behind the business. It organizes your narrative, knowledge base, prompts, workflows, and customer routes so the system can actually be used.
What is Black Crown?
Black Crown is the governance and protection layer. It supports IP boundaries, team persona structure, policy logic, and operating controls.
How do I get started?
Start with the Start Here page, Foundation Snapshot, or Crown Consult 30 if you need help choosing the right path before buying a larger build.
Do you offer refunds?
Due to the digital nature of the products, services, and implementation work, sales are final unless a specific written agreement states otherwise. Results vary based on implementation, access, and follow-through.
Where are you located?
Self Entourage serves customers digitally and offers appointment-based meetings in the Denver metropolitan area when appropriate.
