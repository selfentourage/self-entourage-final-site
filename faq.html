Frequently asked questions

Your questions about Self Entourage, our systems and how to work with us.

What is Self Entourage?
Self Entourage is a consultancy that teaches AI clearly, builds sustainable Brand OS systems and helps you deploy them into the world.
What is Truth OS?
Truth OS is a custom platform for managing your knowledge base, prompts and automations. It unifies your digital presence and anchors your narrative.
What is Black Crown?
Black Crown is a dedicated portal that works alongside Truth OS to create immersive, compliant experiences for your audience.
How do I get started?
Head over to our Start Here page to choose your path. For quick questions, book a 15‑minute consultation.
Do you offer refunds?
Due to the digital nature of our services and products, all sales are final. Results vary based on your implementation and effort.
Where are you located?
We are based in Aurora, Colorado. Visits are by appointment only; please schedule a consultation before travelling.
